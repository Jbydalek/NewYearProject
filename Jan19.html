Gitai no on'na (A Woman with a Mimicry)

by Otosuke

Another day, another dom.

Full disclosure, this is the first manga I’ve ever translated and typeset, so if it looks a bit shit, now you know why. Honestly, with how long it took me to do, I was expecting some sort of stalk home syndrome, but as of right now, I’m over the moon about this one, and let me run by you why.

First off, the art and character interactions are amazing. The main girl is adorably drawn, with a fun personality to follow. A manga where a high and mighty guy gets knocked down a few pegs isn’t something we see every day, so that was an aspect I loved to see.

So with the art being great and the story and characters being fun, what the hell was my problem? Quite simply, it was too damn short. They had sex for like 5 seconds, and the short page count left me feeling like it could have been expanded upon. I wish we had 3 more pages of reactions and ecstasy, but translators can’t be choosers, I guess.

So, with a heavy heart I give this one a 3 out of 4. Could have made it to 4 if it was given room to breathe.
