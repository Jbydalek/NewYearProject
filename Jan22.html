Hinata NTRism

by kiasa

Dipping the toe into some NTR

Alright boys, its NTR time. At some point I knew I had to start covering some of the more *cough* intense *cough* doujins, so so I wouldn’t have to have jump right into 177013, I picked one that starts off a lot less rapey than the usual fare.

The art on this one is pretty fantastic. The main girl’s hairstyle is adorable, and most importantly, we’re not dealing with an ugly bastard in the leading man role. The intensity leaps right off the page and the expressions give some great tension to the scenes.

The character interactions aren’t my cup of tea, but the thoughts racing through the main chick’s mind are pretty hot and add a lot to the scenes. The main guy’s demeanor is the only thing that throws me out of the book. It feels like some poor justification to rape someone when he seems like the kinda guy who could just get some poon whenever he wants.

Overall, good start to something that I know is going to end in tragedy. 3 out of 4 for me and 4 out of 4 for the side of me that likes NTR, but we don’t let that side out in public.
