Momoyuri Gakuen - Himitsu no Soap-bu R (The Momoyuri Academy Secret Soapland Club R)

by Homura Subaru

Yuri pandemonium is the best kind of pandemonium

Yuri’s great, everyone like yuri. Even as a straight male, the more hot girls on screen is infinitely more preferred than fat bastards. I realize I’ve been featuring a lot of pretty normal stuff thus far, so hopefully I can get some more inclusive works from now to the future.

Any who, this work has what I like to call the “busty baby face” syndrome. In that a lot of the girls look super young but have bodies of supermodels. Its not a problem persay, just something that makes it harder for me personally to get into the comic.

Other than that, the art is great, especially in the sex scenes. The girls are expressive and the soft linework contrasted with the intensity of it all works marvelously.

The main girl has some funny lines, but the rest of them seem to be just sex crazed succubae, not that that doesn’t work wonders for hentai, more that it doesn’t lend itself to amazing character interactions.

Overall, a fun read. 2.5 out of 4.
