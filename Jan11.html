Tsundero 1+2

by Takeda Hiromitsu

Nymphomaniac part 3

It’s a tale as old as time, at least in doujins: sex crazed popular girl that’s ultra-hot but inexplicitly a virgin runs into a shy nerd with a baby arm of a penis and they live happily ever after.

Its an oddly bog-standard setup, and wouldn’t be too great if it wasn’t for one thing: Takeda Hiromitsu draws probably some of the best ahegao in the business. It makes up for a lot when those faces are on point.

The couple is almost indistinguishable between any hentai couple, and even though I’m including both chapters this time around, you’ll be getting about as much story as the other works I’ve featured here

3 out of 4, I’m not gonna lie, it got bumped 1.5 points from the ahegao faces.
