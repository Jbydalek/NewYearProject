Yappari Kimi ga Suki (I Really Love You)

by Mashiro Shirako

This one is just classic vanilla.

Gorg art and cute interactions really carry this one through the finish line. Intense sex scenes punctuate what is really a super cute story.

It’s always nice to see an art style that meshes so well with the subject matter. The characters are soft and adorable without edging into “way to cute what are you doing in a hentai” territory.

Alcohol bringing out people’s cute and loving side always gets me for some reason, but it really works here. It’s just enough to keep you invested in the actions on the page.

Solid 3 out of 4, maybe it’s because I’m drunk, but this one is really cute.
