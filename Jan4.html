Boy Meets Maid

by Batsu

I'm not too into maids, but I like this one

Maids as a concept are kinda overdone in the otaku community. While they're everywhere, I never really appreciated them as much as other seemed to have. Like I get it, maids have to fuck you and they're always super hot, but I never got the feeling both parties enjoyed the interaction

Still, this one is great because they have the maid and the butler fuck instead of some sad fat basturd and the maid. The chemistry between the two works really well, and the art by Batsu, while also not 100% to my tastes is pretty damn good
