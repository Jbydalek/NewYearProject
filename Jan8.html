Gwenpool Etchina sekai ni o jama shimasu

by Bowieknife

Here's something you don’t see every day, a doujin based on a newer comic book character. It's refreshing to see a Japanese artist take a crack a Gwenpoole, being one of the funniest and beautiful comics to come out in the last ten years, and I feel as though they did a good job overall

The cover had me worried, but the artist does such a great job capturing the Japanese duo Gurihiru’s art style that you really wonder if they didn’t draw it themselves. Gwen retains her personality through the piece, which already keeps it a step above most doujins, and while “Creampie Man” isn’t much of a character, he does make a good gag. The TL seemed to be able to make it readable, albeit with some parts feeling hella google translate.

Overall, super hot and fun to read, with good art and sexy poses. It gets a 3 out of 4 from me.
