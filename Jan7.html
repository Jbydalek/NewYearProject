Today Surely

by Rocket Monkey

Ore Monogatari!! but with sex

I mean, that’s just a joke, but the slightly more lewd Ore Monogatari!! is kind of actuate. I enjoyed this one about as much as I could, but it just wasn’t my style. The art is really great for what it’s going for, but Rocket Money tends to draw his girls on the chubbier side, which just isn’t my cup of tea. The story is cute enough, forcing the girl’s hand into being more assertive, and the guy to finally get a stiffy for her. Not my speed, but I appreciate it none the less.

3 out of 4, would probs be a 4 if I liked chubby gals.
