Tsuyudaku

by Gezio

Armpit sex leads to a cute confession story

Well, this is the second day in a row we have a weird fetish as the main crux of the comic I’m presenting. Not even cool hentai fetishes, just normie shit with butt plugs and armpits. I’m not sure whether or not that says more about me or the state of doujins, but I digress.

This is one of those classic stories that is about as hentai as hentai can get when it comes to set up, but the story did make me laugh pretty hard where the main guy said “I was kidding when I fucked your armpit, I’m not really into that” right after cumming all over her shoulder. The funny part wasn’t that the guy said it, but the girl then totally bought it. Otherwise its cute, with the girl getting into what her crush’s kinks as the comic goes on. Boilerplate, but a solid foundation.

That foundation is there for some great art. At first glance it’s well above average, but nothing revolutionary, that is until you get to the armpits. They are rendered amazingly in this, really showing the author’s passion for the subject. Otherwise the girl is cute and the expressions are hot, so what more can you ask for.

This is one that wouldn’t stick out if it wasn’t for the armpit thing, but you can tell its made for that audience, and if you’re into that or you think you might be, you’ll probably adore this one, but for me:

2 out of 4, but for any armpit boys out there, a perfect 4 out of 4.
