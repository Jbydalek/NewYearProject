Azuma Taira Oneesan no Junai Karte (Delivery Lady)

by Lazarus

Agressively average.

It takes a lot to not intrest me in a woman with a pigtail, but they were indeed sucessful. When you dont bring an intresting story, art style, or collection of characters, you're gonna join the pump and dump club.

The art is just so bland and the interactions so trite that it bores rather than excites. I just felt nothing from this one, sorry Lazarus.

I feel bad for shitting on this so hard, when it really was just average, but in the land of hentai, average with ok to bad art is one hell of a boner killer.

1 out of 4, forgot about it already.
