Maou to Himitsu Heya (The Demon Lord and the Secret Room)

by Okayusan

It’s like Maoyu, but with sex instead of trade agreements

Demon girls are pretty hot aren’t they, and when paired up with a good mangaka supplying fun writing and stellar art it’s a winning combo. Luckily this is what we’ve got today.

The tsundere archetype works perfectly for someone who oversees an army and has an embarrassing secret spilled about them. It really works when the MC is having none of her shit and both are super attracted to one another, spurring on a fun dynamic between the two.

The art is also no slouch. I feel like I haven’t had enough twintail gals featured on this site for how much I love them. The expressions are adorable when they need to be and severely sexy when the booty bunkers get spelunkered.

Great read overall. Really cute and sweet. 3.5 out of 4, my demon lord is really that cute.
