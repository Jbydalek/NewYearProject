Mai Senpai to Ichatsukitai (I Wanna Flirt With Mai)

by Oryou

Fun fan fiction.

Much like everyone, I was watching bunny girl last season. It was great and I loved that they delved into the relationship instead of teasing it out forever. It's always nice to see doujins cover any seasonal romance anime, but with a good show you’ve got to worry about the integrity of the characters being upheld in the process of making them fuck, luckily, I think this sticks the landing.

The art is good, curbing the more realistic, kisniver-esque proportions and character designs for a more moe look, with robs Mai of some of her tsun and giving her some more dere.

That extends into her personality, mostly to the story’s detriment. The banter between the MC and her really suffers from a lack of punch that was present in the show.

Still, it all comes together in the end. 3 out of 4, fast times at puberty syndrome high.
