Elf tte iu no wa (The Thing About Elves)

by Makita Yoshiharu

Kinda cute elf story, falls flat.

This one was weird, mainly because the drawings are adorable, the elf girl herself is super cute, but honestly the story fluctuates between boring and kinda gross. It’s not bad, the art alone is enough to make this jack off worthy, but if you decide to read the word bubbles, then it mostly comes off as weirdly creepy most of the time. I mean, NTR is one thing, but when you get elements of that in my vanilla, that’s when we start to have problems.

2 out of 4, art gives it something, but the rest is let down by a lack of originality.
