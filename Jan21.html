Cheer Haru

by Haruhisky

Haruhi is hot as fuck

Figure I’d get that out of the way first. She was probably one of my first anime crushes and would today still rank up there with even the most modern of waifus. As such, I held this doujin to a higher standard to represent the god of this universe, luckily it did not disappoint.

The art work, for starters, is on point. It draws more from the light novel style than the anime, but it mimics it to a tee. That should probably tell you all you need to know about the comic’s art style, but sufficed to say it’s amazing.

The character interactions are about as book accurate as the art style. It feels like this was plucked right out of the light novel, just jumped forward enough to have them properly dating. The parts with Kyon wondering if he’s getting turned on cause Haruhi wants him to or because he’s actually into is pitch perfect.

Honestly what more can I say other than I am fucking in love this. It is the perfect Haruhi doujin, between the story, interactions and art. All glorious.

4 out of 4, cured my melancholy.
