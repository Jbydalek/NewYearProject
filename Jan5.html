Muramatasan no Himitsu (Muramatasans Secret)

by Igumox

We got it boys, the first great doujin of the year.

You know an artist is good when they bring out fetishes in you that you didn’t know existed. Igumox is one of those artists, joining degenerates like homunculus in that weird honor. He has really nice soft lines that complement the intensity in the later pages.

The story is cute as it needs to be, the main chick’s adorable, and the MC is as bland as ever, but the art and expressions make up for the lack of plot with cuteness.

The gimmick of a split tongue is a fun one, and unfortunately doesn’t have a tag on any of the hentai sites. The art absolutely catapults it to my favorite of this year.

4 out of 4, god bless.
