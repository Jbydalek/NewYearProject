Natsu to Jun (Summer and Innocence)

by Jorori

Sometimes, good ol’ vanilla is the best.

Honestly, I don’t normally go for the art style of Jorori, but if the man(woman?) can do one thing, it’s make the fluffiest shit you’ve ever seen. Wholesome hentai seems to be getting fewer and further between, so its always nice to see something like this just hit all those buttons.

The art here is only really to bolster up the incredibly cute story of young love shown to us in the doujin. It’s great. The smile on page fifteen makes my heart melt.

4 out of 4. Another great one for the year.
