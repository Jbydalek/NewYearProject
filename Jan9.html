Memory Impact

by Kouda Tomohiro

This one was fun.

A lot of the charm in this one is the cute agressive gal. She, and by proxy the rest of the doujin, gives me sort of a Haruhi vibe for some reason.

Her being cute and spunky made the whole manga for me, and while the comedy of "I hit you when I get drunk" could wear thin, you're probably jerking off too much to care.

The art style isn't as refined as many of the other works featured on this site, but it's still pretty fun. Hey I nutted, so I can't complain.

2 out of 4, neat.
